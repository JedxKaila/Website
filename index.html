ABOUT US

We were born and raised on the Island of Hawai’i. Its musical culture has fueled our love and passion for music. Together we offer a unique experience as a young duo, with a wide range of sweet vocal harmonies blended with the sounds of acoustic guitar. A mix of classic, contemporary, and Hawaiian songs, our live music is sure to enhance any event - delivering an ambiance tailored to fit every occasion. Parties, graduations or weddings, it would be our honor to share with you our love for music in your special moment.
